# Blinkit Sales Data Analysis 🛒📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on *Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)* of Blinkit's sales data. The goal is to analyze sales performance based on item types, outlet characteristics, and consumer preferences to derive actionable business insights.

## 🛠️ Data Cleaning & Preparation
Before analysis, I performed data cleaning to ensure accuracy:
* Corrected inconsistencies in the *'Item Fat Content'* column (mapped 'LF', 'low fat' to 'Low Fat' and 'reg' to 'Regular').
* Handled basic data types and explored the dataset's structure ($8523$ rows and $11$ columns).

## 📈 Key Business Metrics (KPIs)
I calculated essential business metrics to gauge overall performance:
* *Total Sales:* Overall revenue generated.
* *Average Sales:* Mean sales per transaction.
* *Number of Items Sold:* Total quantity of products moved.
* *Average Rating:* Customer satisfaction score.

## 📊 Visualizations & Insights
The project includes several charts to visualize trends:
* *Total Sales by Fat Content:* A *Pie Chart* showing the dominance of 'Low Fat' vs 'Regular' products.
* *Total Sales by Item Type:* A *Bar Chart* identifying top-performing categories.
* *Outlet Analysis:* Analyzing performance across *Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3* cities.
* *Outlet Establishment Trend:* A *Line Chart* showing sales trends over the years.
